Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about shipping from Nigeria to Germany

How long does shipping from Nigeria to Germany take?

Air freight from Lagos to Germany takes 3–5 business days. Once your package arrives in Germany, DHL forwarding to the rest of Europe typically takes an additional 1–2 days.

What items can I ship from Nigeria to Germany?

We ship documents, clothes, shoes, bags, cosmetics, jewellery, dry herbs, hair extensions, food stuffs, and general cargo. Contact us for any unusual items — we'll advise on customs requirements.

How do I book a shipping slot?

Simply WhatsApp us at +234 916 000 9722, fill in the quote form on this page, or visit our office at 42 Kakawa Street, Lagos Island. We'll confirm your slot and send you the drop-off date.

Do you handle customs clearance?

Yes, absolutely. MO Logistics handles all customs documentation and clearance for your shipment from Nigeria to Germany. You do not need to deal with any paperwork.

How much does it cost to ship from Nigeria to Germany?

Pricing is based on weight and dimensions of your package. Contact us on WhatsApp at +234 916 000 9722 for a free, no-obligation quote — we respond within 1 hour during business hours.

Can you ship to other countries in Europe besides Germany?

Yes. Once your package arrives in Germany, we forward it via DHL Express to any country in Europe — including the UK, France, Netherlands, Austria, Switzerland, and more.

Is MO Logistics officially registered?

Yes. MO Logistics International is a CAC (Corporate Affairs Commission) registered company in Nigeria as of 2026. You can ship with full confidence and legal protection.
